# Case Studies in Cybersecurity Foundations

## Case Study 1: Target Data Breach (2013)
- **Issue:** Attackers gained access through a third-party vendor.
- **Impact:** 40M credit/debit card records stolen.
- **Lesson Learned:** Vendor risk management and network segmentation are crucial.

## Case Study 2: WannaCry Ransomware (2017)
- **Issue:** Exploited unpatched SMB vulnerabilities.
- **Impact:** Affected 200,000+ systems in 150 countries.
- **Lesson Learned:** Regular patch management is essential.

## Case Study 3: Twitter Social Engineering Attack (2020)
- **Issue:** Employees targeted with phishing/social engineering.
- **Impact:** High-profile accounts hijacked.
- **Lesson Learned:** Security awareness training reduces insider threats.
