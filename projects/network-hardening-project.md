# Project: Designing a Secure Password Policy

## Objective
Create and evaluate a password policy that balances usability and security.

## Steps
1. Research NIST password guidelines.
2. Define complexity, length, and rotation requirements.
3. Draft a policy document for a small company.
4. Test the policy with a sample group of users.

## Deliverables
- Written password policy
- Short report on usability vs. security trade-offs
