# Module 5 – Risk Management

## Definitions
- **Risk** = Threat × Vulnerability × Impact
- **Threat** – Potential danger
- **Vulnerability** – Weakness exploitable by threats

## Risk Assessment
1. Identify assets
2. Identify threats/vulnerabilities
3. Assess likelihood & impact
4. Prioritize risks

## Mitigation Strategies
- Avoidance
- Reduction
- Transfer (insurance)
- Acceptance

## Key Insight
Risk management = **continuous process** balancing cost and protection.
