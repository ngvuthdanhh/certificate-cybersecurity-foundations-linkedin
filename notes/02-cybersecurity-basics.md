# Module 2 – Cybersecurity Basics

## CIA Triad
- **Confidentiality** – Prevent unauthorized access
- **Integrity** – Protect against modification
- **Availability** – Ensure systems are usable when needed

## Common Threats
- Malware, phishing, ransomware
- Insider threats
- Social engineering

## Security Principles
- Least privilege
- Defense in depth
- Patch management

## Key Insight
Security starts with a **holistic approach** combining people, process, and technology.
