# Module 6 – Incident Response

## IR Lifecycle
1. **Preparation** – Policies, training, tools
2. **Detection & Analysis** – Identify incident indicators
3. **Containment** – Limit spread of attack
4. **Eradication** – Remove threat completely
5. **Recovery** – Restore systems and services
6. **Lessons Learned** – Post-mortem & improvement

## Example Incidents
- Phishing leading to account compromise
- Malware infection on endpoint
- Unauthorized access to database

## Key Insight
A structured IR plan ensures **faster recovery** and reduced damage.
